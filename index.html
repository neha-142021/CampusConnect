Frequently Asked Questions

How does it work?

Our system uses the magic of Firebase to connect students and teachers effortlessly. Simply log in, select your desired time slot, and voilà!

Is it secure?

Absolutely! We've built our system with the strength of a thousand orchids, ensuring your data is protected like a precious flower in a storm.

Can I cancel bookings?

Of course! Life happens, and we understand. Just navigate to your bookings, click cancel, and free up that slot for someone else.

What if I need help?

Fear not, dear Orchid! Our support team is here for you 24/7, ready to guide you through any booking hurdles with grace and expertise.

Is it user-friendly?

As friendly as a blooming orchid in spring! Our intuitive interface makes booking a breeze for both students and teachers.
